The existing `docs/VAULT_PUBLISHER_INTEGRATION.md` is therefore a proposal/specification, not an operational integration receipt.

## Required interpretation

- Opt-in policy does not itself authorize transmission or create a connector.

- Technical access or credentials do not create authority.
- Restricted records and policy files remain outside any proposed sharing surface.
- Operational claims require executable implementation, tests, authority checks, privacy verification, exact-head validation, and durable receipts.
