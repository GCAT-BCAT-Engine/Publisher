**Summary:**

Anthropic's Fable 5 and Mythos 5 suspension is a governance case study in emergency AI restriction.

One public narrative says the government acted abruptly on insufficiently disclosed evidence.

Another says a trusted partner found a serious jailbreak, Anthropic refused to fix or de-deploy, and the administration acted reluctantly.

The governance issue is not which side is rhetorically stronger.

The issue is how a risk signal becomes evidence, how evidence becomes authority, how authority becomes execution, and whether the public can inspect enough of the transition path to distinguish legitimate emergency containment from opaque overreach.
